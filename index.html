    The Purpose of this Blog

    I haven't really decided what I want to do with this page yet. Going in I had plenty ideas, like a suicide blog with a forum and chat groups, a place to star pictures of my drawings, an online journal, etc. But really I think the best thing is just to gain the skills and knowledge to do with this page what I please. It's up and online and I can change it freely. So really the title "Namdi's Blog" is a placeholder.
